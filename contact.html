Questions & Answers

What are the requirements to open a bank account?

To open a bank account, you need a valid ID card, a tax identification number (if applicable), and an initial deposit depending on the account type.

Can I open a bank account online?

Yes, you can open a bank account online through our official website or mobile application without visiting a branch.

How much is the initial deposit?

The initial deposit varies by account type, starting from IDR 50,000 up to IDR 500,000.

Is my money safe with this bank?

Yes, your funds are secure and protected under the Deposit Insurance Corporation (LPS) in accordance with applicable regulations.

Does the bank provide mobile banking services?

Yes, we offer mobile banking services to make transactions easier anytime and anywhere.

How can I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service via call center, official email, or live chat available on our website.

Are there monthly administration fees?

Yes, monthly administration fees apply and vary depending on the type of account you choose.

What should I do if my ATM card is lost?

Please contact our call center immediately to block your ATM card and visit the nearest branch for a replacement.

Does the bank offer loans or credit services?

Yes, we provide various loan products such as personal loans, business loans, and home loans.

How can I check my account balance?

You can check your balance through ATMs, mobile banking, internet banking, or your passbook.
